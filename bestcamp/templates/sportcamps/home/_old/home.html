OUR SERVICES

Sports medicine
As a part of our training camps organization services, we provide a full variety of all medical treatment and testing, like cross-DNA Professional Sports Genetic Tests, Vo2 Testing, and ergometric assessments conducted by accredited Sports and Exercise Scientists. Also, we provide rehabilitation services, sports massages, and physiotherapy. Active Planet works with rehabilitation clinics all over the world.
Sports management
We offer professional sports management services for all functional areas of sporting, special, and cause-related training camps and sports events. From pre-event planning through post-event analysis, the company can handle all operational, technical, and participatory functions of an event, accounting for team travel plans. We provide best flight options, visa support, top performance sports venues and hotels.
Sports Event organization
Regardless of the type of event you are planning, we can handle every aspect of it. We provide flights, accommodation, sports equipment, transfers, inventory, tents, event personnel, and site management. Many sports venues cooperate with Active Planet. Our sporting event management partners include colleges and universities, National Championships, and Sports federations WorldWide.
Active Holidays & Sport Tours
This service is ideal For runners, hikers, mountaineers, bikers, explorers, families, and pretty much all lovers of the outdoors. We organize active holidays of any type all around the world for small groups. Just tell us about your ideal sports tour and we will manage it!
Individuals tours
Sports are your passion and you can't stand lying by the pool all day? With a list of our hotels with sporting facilities, you can easily make all travel arrangements for your individual training camps abroad.Active Planet will package all the things you need: bike hire, the use of the lap pool, other sports facilities, airport transfers, etc.
