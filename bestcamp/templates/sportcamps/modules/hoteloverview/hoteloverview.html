Pickalbatros Aqua Vista Resort - Hurghada

This modern 4-star all-inclusive Hurghada resort is just 700 m from the Red Sea. Hotel base on all-inclusive concept. It offers a lagoon-style outdoor pool and 3 buffet restaurants.
