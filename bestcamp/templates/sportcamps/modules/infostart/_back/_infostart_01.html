How to start?

Set your travel dates

Shortly after your inquiry, you will receive several non-binding offers for your training camp from which you and your teammates can choose the training camp that is right for you. This service is of course completely free.

Consider all options and select the best one

We only need to know the overall number of participants and travel dates to plan your training camp.

Have you already chosen a country and sports venue? You will receive a customized offer based on your requirements.

Enjoy training camp and achieve your goal

After the booking, you will receive all your travel documents and you can look forward to your training camp. If someone is unable to travel to the training camp, we will make a refund. Our group's manager is always supporting the team on the spot.
